## High-Value Content Plays for SEO

### 1. Salary Calculator (Highest Opportunity Score)

**Target keywords:** chief of staff salary (6,600/mo), fractional coo salary, fractional cmo salary, fractional executive cost
**Format:** Interactive calculator tool with email capture
**Why:** "Chief of staff salary" has the highest opportunity score in the dataset (359,700). Salary calculators generate backlinks, dwell time, and repeat visits.

**Data to include (from 2026 market research):**
- Fractional COO: $8,000–$18,000/month retainer
- Fractional CMO: $8,000–$22,000/month retainer
- Fractional CFO: $8,000–$18,000/month retainer
- Fractional CTO: $10,000–$22,000/month retainer (highest)
- Fractional CRO: $10,000–$20,000/month retainer
- Fractional CHRO: $7,000–$15,000/month retainer

- Hourly rates: $150–$350 depending on role

### 2. "What Does a Fractional COO Do?" Definitive Guide

**Target keywords:** what does a fractional coo do, fractional coo, hire fractional coo
**Format:** 2,000–3,000 word pillar page
**Why:** Informational intent that feeds into your job board pages. Structure for AI citation with definition blocks, comparison tables, FAQ schema.

**Key claims to structure around:**
- Decisions unstuck within 48–72 hours
- Same team produces 20–35% more output without adding headcount
- Company can handle 2–3x operational volume before proportional cost increase
- 72% of CEOs plan to increase fractional executive usage in next 12 months
- Fractional workforce doubled from 60,000 to 120,000 professionals (2022–2024)
- 25% of U.S. businesses now use fractional hiring, projected 35% by end of 2026

### 3. Comparison Pages (Highest AI Citation Rate — ~33%)

**Target keywords:** fractional vs full-time coo, fractional coo platforms, best fractional executive services

**Why:** Comparison articles get cited most by AI search (~33% citation share). Build these as balanced, data-rich pages.

## AI SEO Optimization Checklist

For each page, ensure:

- [ ] Clear definition in first paragraph (40–60 words, self-contained)
- [ ] H2/H3 headings match how people phrase queries
- [ ] Comparison tables for any "X vs Y" content
- [ ] Statistics with cited sources (+37–40% AI citation boost)
- [ ] FAQ section with natural-language questions (add FAQPage schema)
- [ ] Author attribution with credentials
- [ ] "Last updated" date visible
- [ ] robots.txt allows GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended
- [ ] Article/JobPosting schema markup on all pages
- [ ] No gated content on pillar pages
